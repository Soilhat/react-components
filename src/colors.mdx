# Color Palette

Here are listed colors used. Refer only to colox box background colors.
Feel free to override colors as per [Tailwind CSS theme set up](https://tailwindcss.com/docs/theme)

## üìù Text and Surface Colors (Dynamic Theme)

These colors are critical for readability and must switch between light and dark modes.

<div className="grid grid-cols-6 pb-2 gap-4">
  <ColorBox colorName="surface-base" className="bg-surface-base text-black" />
  <ColorBox colorName="surface-base-dark" className="bg-surface-base-dark text-white" />
  <div className="col-span-4">
    <h3>Surface base</h3>
    <p>Page or main section background.</p>
    <p>
      <b>Canvas</b>: The broadest background color.
    </p>
  </div>
</div>
<div className="grid grid-cols-6 pb-2 gap-4">
  <ColorBox colorName="surface-panel" className="bg-surface-panel text-black" />
  <ColorBox colorName="surface-panel-dark" className="bg-surface-panel-dark text-white" />
  <div className="col-span-4">
    <h3>Surface panel</h3>
    <p>Background for cards, modals, side panels.</p>
    <p>
      <b>Elevation</b>: Should be slightly darker than surface-base in light mode, and slightly lighter in dark mode to
      create a "floating" effect.
    </p>
  </div>
</div>
<div className="grid grid-cols-6 pb-2 gap-4">
  <ColorBox colorName="text-primary" className="bg-text-primary text-white" />
  <ColorBox colorName="text-primary-dark" className="bg-text-primary-dark" />
  <div className="col-span-4">
    <h3>Text primary</h3>
    <p>Main body text, H1/H2 headings.</p>
    <p>
      <b>Readability</b>: The darkest/lightest text color.
    </p>
  </div>
</div>
<div className="grid grid-cols-6 pb-2 gap-4">
  <ColorBox colorName="text-secondary" className="bg-text-secondary" />
  <ColorBox colorName="text-secondary-dark" className="bg-text-secondary-dark" />
  <div className="col-span-4">
    <h3>Text secondary</h3>
    <p>Captions, helper text, form field placeholders.</p>
    <p>
      <b>Details/Subtlety</b>: Lower contrast than primary text.
    </p>
  </div>
</div>
<div className="grid grid-cols-6 pb-2 gap-4">
  <ColorBox colorName="text-on-primary" className="bg-text-on-primary" />
  <ColorBox colorName="text-on-primary-dark" className="bg-text-on-primary-dark text-white" />
  <div className="col-span-4">
    <h3>Text on primary</h3>
    <p>Text or icon placed on a primary background (color-primary)</p>
    <p>
      <b>Contrast</b>: Must ensure AA or AAA contrast ratio with color-primary. Often white or black.
    </p>
  </div>
</div>
